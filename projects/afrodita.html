Afrodita 1.0

Yveete Bash Profile

Afrodita 1.0 is a space settlement that was made for the Nasa Ames contest. It is supposed to be located in the Langrange 4 (Earth-Moon system) and it's consisted of 3 toruses which provide pseudo gravity to accommodate ∼ 30 000 inhabitants.

Afrodita 1.0 was a project in the NASA Ames Space Settlement Contest 2016 that received the 3rd place. The 90-paged project was written by Maria Disli, Andrei Lu, Filip Gkogkos, George Valtas and Nicolas Mantescu
