What is Watch Space,

Watch Space is an html site that allows people to see who and what is in space. More precisely, the astronauts and the location of the ISS. Watch Space uses JavaScript and mostly jQuery to obtain information from various JSON files on the internet [r1].

General Comments,

When i started to develop the website, there were 5 people in space. Once I made it online there were 3 people. I really hope that in the next years there will be more astronauts in space but not too many so that my list on the website will overflow ¯\_(ツ)_/¯.
